## Description

Dropship Tools is a CLI + web dashboard for dropshippers who want to validate products with real data before investing.

Stop picking products by gut feel. Dropship Tools pulls AliExpress pricing, calculates your true margins (including platform fees, shipping, and ad spend), and scores competition across Google Trends and TikTok — all from your terminal.

**Key Features:**
- AliExpress pricing & supplier lookup
- Margin calculator (COGS, platform fees, ad spend, markup)
- Competition score via Google Trends + TikTok analysis
- AI-generated product listings for TikTok Shop & Shopify
- Performance Dashboard to track products at scale

**Who it's for:** Dropshippers validating new products before committing budget.

**Pricing:** $29/mo — 7-day free trial, no credit card required to start.
